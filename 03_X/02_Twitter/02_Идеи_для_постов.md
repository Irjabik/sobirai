   **Черновик поста:**
   I don't start my day from timeline noise.
   
   I start from 1 private list for 10-15 min.
   
   My simple flow:
   - Check who posted in last 12-24h
   - Leave 5-7 useful replies (not "gm")
   - Save 2 strong tweets for later quote/reply
   - Send 1 short DM after real value exchange
   
   Why this works:
   - less chaos
   - better relationships
   - higher signal on CT
   
   Private lists are underrated networking OS for CMs.
