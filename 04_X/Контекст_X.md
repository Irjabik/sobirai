## 4) “Анти-AI” принципы (чтобы посты не выглядели сгенерированными)

Мои длинные статьи могли быть написаны с помощью ИИ. Поэтому задача — сохранять идеи, но делать текст **человечным**.

### Признаки “ИИ-налета”, которых избегать
- Слишком гладкий, одинаковый ритм по всему тексту
- Слишком много абстракций и “больших слов” без личной детали
- Длинные абзацы без воздуха
- Слишком универсальные “истины” без конкретного действия/наблюдения
- Полотна текста, которые невозможно читать
- Картинки плохого качества, особенно с водяным знаком ИИ

### Формула “человечного” твита (минимум 2 из 5)
Добавляй в каждый твит минимум **2 блока**:
1) **Story**: 1 сцена / 1 момент  
2) **Progress**: что сделал(а) / чему научился  
3) **Proof**: цифра/метрика/правило  
4) **People**: кого нашел/кому благодарен/кого читаю  
5) **Prompt**: вопрос / “show me yours”

## 7) Чеклист качества перед публикацией

- **Первый экран**: есть хук или конкретика в первых 1–2 строках
- **Одна мысль**: твит не пытается сказать все сразу
- **Воздух**: короткие строки, не монолитный блок
- **Конкретика**: добавлена 1 деталь/правило/цифра
- **Повод ответить**: вопрос или “show me yours”
- **Без “ИИ-налета”**: меньше абстракций, больше живого наблюдения
- **Читабельность**: легко читается, без “полотен”
- **Визуал (если есть)**: без водяных знаков ИИ, нормальное качество

### Статья (про “life-change” и вероятность)

Most strategies for finding a life-change are mathematical suicide
You are desperately waiting for that one life-changing event to make you rich, yet you spend 99% of your time hiding in the exact places where luck is mathematically impossible to find.
Nassim Taleb, a former Wall Street options trader and one of the most brilliant modern analysts of risk, taught the world to fear unpredictability and market crashes.
Nassim Taleb
But he also revealed the fundamental mechanism of making chaos your greatest ally in wealth creation
The secret is continuously testing micro-hypotheses with asymmetric upside potential.
Suppose the statistical chance of massive success for any of your unconventional ideas is just 1.4%.
If you try to execute your plan exactly 1 time, you are mathematically guaranteed to fail.
But if you make 342 independent attempts, the probability of at least one breakthrough success skyrockets to 99.1%
Math is ruthless to the lazy, but it is incredibly generous to the relentless


I have a friend who spent months trying to find a solid position and break out of his routine.
Instead of looking for one perfect and safe path, he started hacking the architecture of probability itself.
He relentlessly sent out hundreds of applications for remote positions, collecting rejections daily.
Simultaneously, he launched a tiny, seemingly insignificant micro-business on the side just to test his skills.

This tiny, absurd step became his personal positive black swan.
One of the random clients he served through his micro-business happened to be an influential founder.
The founder loved my friend's proactive approach and his ability to solve unconventional problems efficiently.
The result? 

Job offer as an assistant to a major businessman
The lesson here is crystal clear
You cannot predict where luck will come from, but you can systematically set traps for it.
In the crypto space, the exact same laws of probability apply.
Buying a single meme coin with your entire net worth hoping for a 100x is playing roulette with a negative mathematical expectancy.
But consistently growing your Twitter, actively working in the Web3 trenches, building authentic networking connections and studying AI architecture etc - that is a radical expansion of your luck funnel.
You have no idea which of your endeavors might eventually lead to a massive, life changing breakthrough.
And the beauty of it is that you don't need to know.
A bad investor tries to guess the exact direction of the macro trend.
A good investor strictly follows the trend with disciplined risk management.
A brilliant investor sets 100 asymmetrical traps for luck across different ecosystems and goes to sleep peacefully.
This is the classic barbell strategy in action.
You must keep 90% of your resources both time and capital - hyper-safe.
Then, you distribute the remaining 10% across dozens of high-risk, potentially explosive ideas.
If these high-risk bets fail, your maximum loss is strictly capped at that 10%, and your foundation remains entirely unshaken.
But if just one of those micro-bets triggers a positive black swan event, the upside is practically infinite.
Formatting your life and your portfolio this way makes you truly antifragile.
Chaos becomes your friend
Most people fail because they experience cognitive friction after the first few failed attempts and simply give up.
Every rejection, every fail, every ignored cold email is just crossing off a number on your way to that 99.1% statistical certainty of success.
Luck is not a magical aura reserved for the chosen few.
